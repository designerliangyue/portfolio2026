# Project Overview
Ignite is a multi-functional platform marketed in 4 countries of Southeast Asia, which provides an end-to-end insurance online selling and management journey, and a place for professional growth as well as team building. 
<columns>
	<column>
		### My Role
		UX researcher
		Product designer
	</column>
	<column>
		### Responsibilities
		Conducted stakeholder interviews, user interviews, and competitive research.
		Built wireframes and lo-fi prototype to test ideas.
		Made hi-fi design and visual elements.
		Collaborated with team to make sure the app goes live as expected.
	</column>

## Interviews
<callout icon="💡">
	**INSIGHTS from the online interview with 10 users in Indonesia and 15 in Vietnam.**
	**TRUST** is a big asset as an insurance agent.
	**QUALITY** of service is key to differentiate from other platform that offer similar products for agent to sell.
	Most agents **SPECIALIZE** in only 2 or 3 types of insurance products.
	Selling complex insurance product requires **HEAVY** training.
</callout>
